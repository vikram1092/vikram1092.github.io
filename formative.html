iOS For Beginners


Formative Assessment

Overview

The nature of this introductory course would be cumulative, as each lesson will build upon the knowledge gained in the previous lesson. For example: Lesson 1 would require a student to make an application with one screen. Lesson 2 would teach the student to create labels and buttons. Lesson 3 would teach the student how to data models in views. Since the learning is inherently cumulative, formative tasks will be present at the end of each lesson to encourage users to be creative with what they just learned in each lesson. Students will be encouraged to create something of their own using their current state of knowledge. This deepens understanding of content and strengthens the base for each increment in the course.

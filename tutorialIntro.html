Downloading XCode

To get started on developing iOS applications, you'll need a few things.

  • A Macbook
  • The Xcode Application
  • An Apple Developer Certification


We will first focus on downloading Apple's own Integrated Development Environment (IDE) called XCode, which will allow us to create our own mobile applications.
Go to the App Store on your Macbook, and Search for XCode.

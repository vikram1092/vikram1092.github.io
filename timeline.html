FAQ


What does the team look like that will work on this?

The team will be comprised of iOS developers and web developers.


What industry you are working in?

This tutorial will be in the iOS app development industry.


What is the topic of the training that you will need to develop?

The topic of this tutorial will be an introduction to the iOS development environment and practices.


How many modules will be completed?

There will be 6 modules of training.


Who will your Subject Matter Expert(s) be?

My SME’s will be experienced iOS developers who have extensive knowledge of the environment and system.


What is outcome for the training?

The outcome for the training is for students to develop rudimentary applications with a small variety of feature sets.


How will participants be assessed?

Participants will be assessed based on their ability to perform the tasks being taught. Each module will comprise of a task necessary to be completed in order to progress, as the knowledge will build on the previous module.


What content already exists?

There are plenty of iOS development training modules available online. Each training module assumes students’ experience levels, comfort, and ability to keep up pace, so students have a variety to choose from.


What content needs to be created - and what will the format be like?

Detailed and slow-paced training needs to be created for beginner iOS developers, and the format must be through multimedia, pictures, and text, so that individuals may be able to follow at their own pace.


How long will it take for students to complete the training module once it is developed?

Varying experience levels may find it easier or harder. My estimate for completion of the 6 modules would be anywhere from 1 day to 5 days.

What additional resources are required?

Since this is an iOS development course, a Macbook, an internet connection, and a testing device (such as an iPhone or iPad) are required.
